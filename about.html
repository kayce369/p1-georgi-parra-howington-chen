About Buzzy Bee

Relax, Sip, Chat. . .

Buzzy Bee is owned and operated by three graduate students at the University of Florida. While their passion is web design, running a coffee shop is their outlet. The open and clean feeling of Buzzy Bee welcomes you to study, work, chat, or read. Grab a drink, connect to our FREE wifi, and enjoy the atomosphere!

The hive from which we came

Buzzy Bee Coffee Shop is located in downtown Gainseville, Florida.

Buzzing Beginnings

From a group project turned business, the Buzzy Bee coffee shop was born in 2021. Our goal was and is to create a comfortable and safe place for graduate students to come and study.
